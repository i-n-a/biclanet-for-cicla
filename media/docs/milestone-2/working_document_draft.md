Project I – Mestrado em Computação Criativa e Inteligência Artificial (MCIA001N01)
Course: Master’s in Creative Computing and Artificial Intelligence
This project integrates knowledge and skills acquired across four key disciplines:
Automatic Applied Learning
Physical Computing and Internet of Things
Introduction to Artificial Intelligence
Creative Programming
The work is developed in collaboration with teams from three master’s programs (including two programs besides our own). Our team is responsible for delivering an engineering and technical solution that supports two independent projects from the partner groups.
After analyzing the concepts, requirements, and design proposals from both partner teams (Group A and Group B), we identified the need for a single, reusable white-label hardware-software solution. This approach allows us to concentrate our efforts on developing one modular, programmable device that can be seamlessly integrated into both projects with minimal or no modifications.
The core technical contribution is the Universal Capture and Fixation Module (UCFM) — a versatile electro-mechanical system designed to securely capture and hold bicycles. Despite the differing artistic and interaction concepts of the two partner projects, the UCFM satisfies the shared functional requirement: providing a reliable, safe, and automated mechanism for urban on-street bicycle storage and security.
By creating this universal module, we ensure efficient resource use, maintain consistency across implementations, and enable each partner team to focus on their unique creative and user-experience vision while relying on a robust and adaptable technical foundation.
